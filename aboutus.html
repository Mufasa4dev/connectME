About ConnectMe

Welcome to ConnectMe! where we connect young Nigerians to professional and vocational opportunities;
we provide a credible process for potential employers to get the best employees and a platform
where SMEs can meet potential consumers.

Our Vision

To reduce youth unemployement in Nigeria by 30% and provide opportunities for the disabled.

Our Mission

To provide a platform for youth professional and vocational skill upgrade and connect them to opportunities.

Who Are We

ConnectMe is a digital platform with a vision to reduce youth unemployment in Nigeria by skilling up learned youths through our means of availble trainings, workshops and job matching.
Also, we provide a platform where small and medium scale entrepreneurs can showcase their businesses and get items with particular attention to the disabled. As such, we are geared to advancing the Sustainable Development Goal 8 - Decent Work and Economic Growth; which aims to promote sustained, inclusive and sustainable economic growth, full and productive employment and decent work for all.
