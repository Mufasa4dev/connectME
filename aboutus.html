    Who are we?

    ConnectMe is an organization with the vision to reduce youth unemployment in Nigeria by skilling up learned
    and unlearned youths via our available trainings,workshops and job opportunities.
    As such we are geared towards advancing the sustainable development goal 8
    -Decent work and Economic Growth- which aims to promote inclusive and sustainable
    full and productive employment and decent work fo all.
