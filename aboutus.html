AboutConnectMe

Welcome to ConnectMe! where we connect young Nigerians to professional and vocational opportunities;
we provide a credible process for potential employers to get the best employees and a platform
where SMEs can meet potential consumers.

Our Vision

To create sustainable economic prosperity for all.

Our Mission

To provide a platform for youth professional and vocational skill upgrade and connect them to opportunities.

Who Are We

ConnectMe is a digital platform with a vision to reduce youth unemployment in Nigeria by skilling up learned and
unlearned youth through our means of availble trainings,workshops and job matching. Also, we provide a platform
where small and medium scale entrepreneurs can showcase their businesses and get items with particular attention
to the disabled. As such, we are geared to advancing the Sustainable Development Goal 8 - Decent Work and Economic
Growth; which aims to promote sustained, inclusive and sustainable economic growth, full and productive employment
and decent work for all.
