AboutConnectMe

ConnectMe is a digital platform with a vision to reduce youth unemployment(Physically Challenged Persons Inclusive) in Nigeria by skilling up learned and unlearned youths through our means of available trainings, internships and job matching; Connecting Nigerians to professionals and Vocational Opportunities. Also we provide a platform where small and medium entrepreneurs can showcase their businesses.

Our Vision

To create economic empowerment opportunities for 6Million Nigerian Youths for a period of 10 years

Our Mission

We provide a platform to upgrade youths with professional and vocational skills, connect them to opportunities, as well as create a thriving space for SMEs to meet potential consumers.
