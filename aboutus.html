AboutConnectMe

ConnectMe is a digital platform with a vision to reduce youth unemployment (Physically challenged Persons Inclusively) in Nigeria by skilling learned and unlearned youths through our means of available trainings, internship and job matching; Connecting Nigerians to Professional and Vocational Opportunities. We also provide a platform where Small and Medium Entrepreneurs (SMEs) can showcase their businesses.

Our Vision

To create economic empowerment opportunities for 6 million Nigerian Youths for a period of 10 years.

Our Mission

To provide a platform that upgrade youths with professional and vocational skills, connect them to opportunities, as well as create a thriving space for SMEs to meet potential consumers.
